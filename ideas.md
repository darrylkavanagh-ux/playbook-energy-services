# Selected Design: Neo-Brutalist Energy

**Design Philosophy:**
We are building a tool that screams "STOP OVERPAYING." It needs to be loud, urgent, and utilitarian. The design treats high energy bills as a hazard that needs immediate remediation.

**Core Principles:**
1.  **Urgency & Warning:** Energy bills are a crisis. The design treats high prices as a hazard.
2.  **Raw Utility:** No decoration. Every element is a tool or a label.
3.  **High Visibility:** Maximum contrast for readability and impact.

**Color Palette:**
*   **Primary:** Safety Yellow (`#FFD700`) - The color of hazard tape and warning signs.
*   **Contrast:** Construction Black (`#000000`) - For deep contrast and structure.
*   **Background:** Off-White / Paper (`#F5F5F5`) - To keep it readable but not sterile.
*   **Accent:** Signal White (`#FFFFFF`) - For high-contrast text areas inside black blocks.

**Typography:**
*   **Headings:** `Space Grotesk` (Bold/Black) - Geometric, quirky, and authoritative.
*   **Body:** `Space Mono` or `JetBrains Mono` - Monospaced font implies raw data, transparency, and "no hidden print".

**UI Elements:**
*   **Hard Borders:** 4px solid black borders on everything. No soft shadows.
*   **Hard Shadows:** `box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px #000000`. Buttons feel physical and heavy.
*   **Marquee Text:** Scrolling tickers for "LIVE MARKET RATES" or "URGENT SAVINGS".
*   **Asymmetric Grid:** Layouts that break the center-alignment to create tension and interest.

**Visual Assets Needed:**
1.  **Hero Image:** A gritty, high-contrast image of power lines or an electric meter, perhaps treated with a halftone or dither effect to look "printed" or "lo-fi".
2.  **Icons:** Thick-lined, angular icons (lightning bolt, receipt, hazard triangle).
3.  **Texture:** Subtle "concrete" or "paper grain" noise overlay to reduce the digital sterility.
