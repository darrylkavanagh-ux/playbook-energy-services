## 3. LIABILITY ANALYSIS

### A. Liability of Cavan General Hospital (HSE)
**Breach of Duty:**
*   **Misdiagnosis:** Failure to rule out differential diagnoses for thunderclap headache (SAH/Dissection) before diagnosing migraine.
*   **Contraindicated Prescription:** Prescribing a vasoconstrictor (Sumatriptan) to a patient with red-flag symptoms of vascular injury. This is a "Never Event" level of error.
*   **Causation:** "But for" the administration of the vasoconstrictor, the dissection may have healed spontaneously or been managed with anticoagulants, preventing the stroke.

### B. Liability of Mr. Anthony Mulvaney
**Breach of Duty:**
*   **Battery/Assault:** Treatment performed without valid consent (failure to warn of material risk of stroke - *Montgomery* standard).
*   **Negligence:** Performing manipulation on a patient with potential vascular compromise without screening.
*   **Causation:** The manipulation was the "precipitating event" that caused the initial intimal tear in the vertebral artery.
