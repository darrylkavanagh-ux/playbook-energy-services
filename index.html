Audit Period
We can audit up to six years of historical billing. The longer the period, the greater the potential recovery. Most clients select the full six years to maximise the retrospective claim.
The statutory recovery period is six years. We recommend selecting the maximum.
Why six years?

The Statute of Limitations in Ireland and the UK allows recovery of overcharges going back six years from the date of discovery. Selecting the maximum period gives Foxlite the widest possible window to identify and recover accumulated overcharges. There is no additional cost for auditing the full period.
