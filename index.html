The Problem

Businesses lose millions to hidden overcharges every year.

Energy bills, waste management, banking fees, telecoms, insurance, and property costs often contain systematic errors, legacy tariffs, and contractual overcharges that go undetected for years. Most organisations simply don't have the specialist expertise or time to audit every line item across every supplier.

How It Works

Six steps to recovered savings.

No disruption to your business. No upfront cost. We manage the entire process from initial review to recovered funds.

Free Review

Send us a sample of recent bills. Our team conducts a preliminary assessment to identify potential overcharges and estimate the scale of recovery opportunity.

Letter of Authority

A simple, one-page document authorising us to request billing data directly from your suppliers. No commitment, no obligation at this stage.

Data Collection

We request complete billing histories from each supplier for the full six-year statutory period. All data handling is GDPR compliant.

Forensic Audit

Our specialists conduct a line-by-line examination of every bill, cross-referencing tariff rates, contract terms, regulatory requirements, and supplier calculations.

Recovery & Negotiation

We present findings to each supplier with a formal claim for refund. We handle all correspondence, escalation, and if necessary, regulatory complaint.

Ongoing Monitoring

After recovery, we continue to monitor your accounts to ensure correct billing going forward and capture any future savings opportunities.
