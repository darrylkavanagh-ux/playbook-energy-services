# Code of Conduct
**Playbook Corporation Limited**

All contributors to this platform agree to conduct themselves professionally,
lawfully, and with integrity at all times.

## Our Standards
- Professional conduct in all interactions
- Respect for confidentiality — this codebase handles sensitive legal evidence
- Zero tolerance for any attempt to misuse, exfiltrate, or compromise platform data
- Compliance with all applicable laws including GDPR, POCA 2002, and the Computer Misuse Act 1990

## Enforcement
Violations will result in immediate access revocation and may result in
legal action. Contact: legal@playbook-corp.com
