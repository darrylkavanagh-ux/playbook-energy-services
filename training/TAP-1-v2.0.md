## PART A: DATA INTEGRITY RULES

### RULE 1 — MINIMUM TWO-SOURCE VERIFICATION (SENTINEL GRADE V6+)

Every "PRICE NOW" or "CURRENT" must be confirmed by minimum TWO independent data sources.

- 1 source: Label "SINGLE SOURCE, UNCONFIRMED" — do NOT use for scenario confirmation or entry-level
- 0 sources: State "Data unavailable"

**SENTINEL V-GRADE ASSIGNMENT:**
- V7: 3+ sources agree within 10 pips/$0.50 — CONFIRMED
- V6: 2 sources agree within 15 pips/$1.00 — CORROBORATED
- V5: 1 source only — SINGLE SOURCE flag
- V4: Sources disagree >20 pips/$2.00 — CONFLICTED

**Source Categories:**
- A: Aggregators (TradingEconomics, Investing.com, Yahoo)
- B: Specialist (TradingView, OANDA, Forex Factory)
- C: API feeds (Alpha Vantage, TwelveData, FRED)
- D: News wire (Reuters, Bloomberg, MarketWatch)

Minimum: 1 from Category A + 1 from any other.

### RULE 2 — OTC / WEEKEND DATA QUARANTINE

All data outside regulated market hours: "[PRICE] — OTC/WEEKEND INDICATION — NOT A REGULATED MARKET PRICE"

OTC data is AWARENESS ONLY. NEVER used for: scenario weighting, entry calculation, stop/TP, scenario confirmation, R:R, or "PRICE NOW" field.

If regulated market not yet open: widen all scenario ranges by 50%, reduce confidence by 10 percentage points.

### RULE 3 — INTRADAY LOW vs PREVAILING PRICE

"PRICE NOW" = prevailing trading level confirmed by 2+ sources within 15 minutes. Intraday extremes reported SEPARATELY.

## PART B: SCENARIO FRAMEWORK RULES

### RULE 4 — SCENARIO CONFIRMATION CRITERIA

- **CONFIRMED:** ALL criteria met with V6+ verified data.
- **INDICATED:** Majority met. Confidence -15%. Position size 15%.
- **UNCONFIRMED:** <50% met or V4/V5 data. Signal: FLAT.
- **NO MATCH:** Outside all scenarios. Signal: FLAT. Log as TRAINING EVENT.

### RULE 5 — THE "PRICED IN" FILTER

Before any crisis-event forecast:
1. Price at START of crisis build-up
2. Price at LAST CLOSE before event
3. PRE-EVENT MOVE = Step 1 - Step 2
4. ANALOGUE TOTAL MOVE from THE ORB database
5. PRICED_IN% = (PRE-EVENT MOVE / ANALOGUE TOTAL) × 100
6. FORECAST REMAINING = ANALOGUE TOTAL × (1 - PRICED_IN%)

- >75%: ⚠️ SIGNIFICANT PRIOR PRICING
- >100%: Counter-scenario min 30%, bearish continuation capped 40%

**AUDIT EXAMPLE:** Jan 27 start 1.2016 → Feb 28 close 1.1817 = 199 pips pre-event. June 2025 analogue 160 pips. Priced-in: 124% — EXCEEDED.

### RULE 6 — COUNTER-ARGUMENT MINIMUM WEIGHT

Every forecast: structured counter-argument in MAIN BODY with own entry, stop, TP, R:R, trigger. Minimum 25% probability. Anticipated events: "sell the fact" minimum 30%.

### RULE 7 — ESCALATION BIAS CIRCUIT BREAKER

Market test before any escalation:
- Q1: Market moved in direction implied? NO → BLOCKED
- Q2: Anticipated or surprise? ANTICIPATED → 50% discount
- Q3: Risk grade increase >2.0 in 24hrs → CIRCUIT BREAKER

## PART C: OIL TRANSMISSION MODEL RULES

### RULE 8 — SUSTAINED vs SPIKE OIL DISTINCTION

- **CAT 1 (5+ days):** Full regression: +10% Brent = -50 to -100 pips EUR/USD
- **CAT 2 (1-4 days):** Reduced: +10% Brent = -25 to -50 pips EUR/USD
- **CAT 3 (intraday/OTC):** Model NOT applicable. NOISE not SIGNAL.

## PART E: CONFIDENCE CALIBRATION

### RULE 10 — DIRECTIONAL CONFIDENCE FRAMEWORK

- 90-100%: Trend continuation ONLY. NEVER crisis events.
- 70-89%: 5+ analogues, priced-in <50%
- 50-69%: Mixed signals, 3-4 analogues, priced-in 50-75%
- Below 55% = FLAT signal
- Below 45% = FLAT + explicit statement

**Automatic Reductions (cumulative):**
- Weekend event: -10%
- Priced-in >75%: -15%
- OTC data used: -10%
- Single source for core price: -10%
- No close analogue: -15%
- Cumulative escalation >2.0 pts in 24hrs: -10%

### RULE 11 — BIAS DETECTION & CORRECTION

**CURRENT STATUS: BEARISH OVERESTIMATION BIAS ACTIVE (2/2 forecasts)**

Corrections until 2 consecutive forecasts score >6.5/10:
- All bearish targets moved 30% closer to current price
- Counter-argument (bullish) probability +10%
- "BIAS CORRECTION ACTIVE" flag in forecast header
