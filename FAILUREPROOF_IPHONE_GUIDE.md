## 🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING

### 😰 PROBLEM 1: Can't Tap Pencil Icon (Step 3D)

**The pencil ✏️ icon doesn't work when you tap it**

**TRY THESE FIXES:**

**Fix 1: Zoom In**
1. **Pinch with two fingers** (spread apart) to zoom in
2. **Make the pencil icon larger**
3. **Try tapping it again**

**Fix 2: Tap and Hold**
1. **Tap and HOLD** the pencil icon for 1 second
2. See if menu appears or field becomes editable

**Fix 3: Tap the Field Itself**
1. **Tap directly on the** `./` **text** instead of pencil
2. Field might become editable

**Fix 4: Landscape Mode**
1. **Turn your iPhone sideways** (landscape)
2. **Screen layout changes**
3. **Try tapping pencil again**

**Fix 5: Restart**
1. **Close Safari completely** (swipe up, close app)
2. **Wait 10 seconds**
3. **Open Safari again**
4. **Go to:** https://vercel.com/new
5. **Start from STEP 2A again**

---
