Proprietary Databases

Five proprietary databases underpin every Foxlite forensic engagement. Built and maintained exclusively by Foxlite — not available to any competitor.

🗄️

FBMD — Forensic Bill Matrix Database

Proprietary · Deployed

The core Foxlite database. Stores and cross-references every billing line item across 6-year lookback windows. Contains normalised rate tables for all 38 Irish utility, telecoms, waste, banking, and insurance providers. Every client bill is ingested, parsed, and matched against 12,000+ known error signatures.

Deployed in:
Energy auditsWaste auditsTelecoms auditsBanking charge reviewsInsurance audits

IRMD — Irish Rate Market Database

Proprietary · Live-Synced

Real-time market rate repository tracking electricity, gas, and commodity pricing from CRU, Eurostat, SEAI, and direct supplier feeds. Updated weekly. Used to benchmark every client tariff against the prevailing market — identifying overcharges that arise from rate drift, outdated contracts, or supplier error.

Forensic Methodologies

Five proprietary investigation methodologies — developed and refined across 200+ Irish forensic audit engagements since 2019.

🔬

Forensic Bill Matrix (FBM) Protocol

Core Methodology · Proprietary

The primary Foxlite audit process. Every invoice across the lookback period is ingested, normalised, and loaded into the FBMD. Each billing line is then cross-referenced against: (1) contracted rates, (2) live market rates, (3) regulatory schedules, (4) supplier published tariffs, (5) VAT and levy rules. Discrepancies are categorised, valued, and escalated by materiality threshold.

Process Steps:
  1. 1.Document collection: 6-year invoice archive, contract documents, supplier correspondence
  2. 2.Data normalisation: parse all bills to standardised FBMD format
  3. 3.Rate cross-reference: match each unit rate against IRMD market benchmarks
  4. 4.Regulatory overlay: apply RCRD schedules to identify exemption gaps
  5. 5.Error signature matching: compare against 340+ SEID error patterns
  6. 6.Materiality ranking: triage findings by recovery value
  7. 7.Evidence compilation: prepare legal-grade dispute documentation
Deployed in:
EnergyWasteTelecomsBankingInsuranceFleetProperty
📈

Parallel Period Comparison (PPC)

Statistical Methodology · Proprietary

Compares billing across equivalent periods (same quarter, prior year) to isolate anomalous spikes not explained by usage patterns or tariff changes. Eliminates seasonal and volumetric noise to reveal systematic overcharging. Particularly effective for detecting standing charge inflation and capacity band errors.

Process Steps:
  1. 1.Index billing periods for seasonal normalisation
  2. 2.Extract like-for-like consumption comparisons
  3. 3.Model expected spend against usage data
  4. 4.Identify statistically significant deviations (>2σ)
  5. 5.Attribute causation: rate error vs usage vs contract
Deployed in:
EnergyTelecomsWaterWaste
📋

Contractual Entitlement Audit (CEA)

Legal-Grade Methodology · Proprietary

Systematic review of all supplier contracts, tariff agreements, and service level documents against actual billing. Identifies cases where clients have been billed outside contracted terms — either through tariff migration without consent, contract expiry billing, or unilateral charge introduction. All findings are documented to dispute-standard evidence quality.

Process Steps:
  1. 1.Extract all contracted rates, terms, and conditions
  2. 2.Map contract clauses to actual billing categories
  3. 3.Identify periods of non-compliant billing
  4. 4.Quantify total overcharge from contract deviation
  5. 5.Draft formal dispute correspondence with legal references
Deployed in:
BankingTelecomsEnergyFleetInsurance
🏛️

Regulatory Entitlement Review (RER)

Compliance Methodology · Proprietary

Proactive review of all applicable regulatory exemptions, rebates, and protections the client is legally entitled to but may not be receiving. Covers CRU-mandated protections, SEAI energy efficiency grants, CCL (Climate Change Levy) exemptions, ComReg billing standards, EPA waste levy exemptions, Revenue VAT categories, and Central Bank SME lending codes.

Process Steps:
  1. 1.Client profile mapping to applicable regulatory frameworks
  2. 2.Entitlement gap analysis (current vs entitled)
  3. 3.Backdating recoverable entitlements under relevant statutes
  4. 4.Preparation of formal regulatory submissions
  5. 5.Monitoring of ongoing compliance and enforcement
Deployed in:
All verticals — energy, waste, banking, telecoms, insurance, fleet
🔗

Multi-Category Forensic Cross-Audit (MCFCA)

Advanced Methodology · Proprietary

Simultaneous forensic investigation across all 8 audit categories for a single client. Uses pattern-matching across categories to identify systemic overcharging that transcends individual supplier relationships — often revealing root causes in contract management failures, procurement weaknesses, or supplier exploitation of billing system complexity.

Process Steps:
  1. 1.Full-spectrum document collection across all 8 categories
  2. 2.Parallel FBM analysis per category
  3. 3.Cross-category correlation analysis
  4. 4.Systemic root cause identification
  5. 5.Unified recovery strategy across all categories
  6. 6.Single consolidated dispute and negotiation process
Deployed in:
Complete Audit serviceMulti-site operatorsPublic sectorHealthcare

Rate Reconciliation Engine (RRE)

Proprietary Algorithm

Automated comparison engine that ingests client billing data and runs concurrent comparisons against contracted rates, IRMD market rates, CRU published tariffs, and supplier price lists. Outputs a reconciliation matrix showing exactly where overbilling occurred, by how much, and across which periods.
