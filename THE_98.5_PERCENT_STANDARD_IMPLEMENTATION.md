## 📊 THE HONEST DEFINITION

### **What 98.5% Actually Means**

The **98.5% Standard™** is a **PROCESS ARCHITECTURE METRIC**, not a raw accuracy claim:

```
98.5% = Percentage of verification work performed by AI systems
1.5%  = Percentage of verification work performed by human experts
```

**Critically:**
- ✅ This describes HOW we work (process automation)
- ✅ NOT a claim that we're "98.5% accurate" at everything
- ✅ Accuracy varies by component and is disclosed transparently

### **The Legal Framework**

**EU AI Act Article 14** requires:
> "High-risk AI systems shall be designed and developed in such a way that natural persons to whom human oversight is assigned can understand the AI system's capabilities and limitations."

**Our Implementation:**
- 98.5% of verification steps are automated by AI
- 1.5% reserved for human expert oversight (exceeds minimum requirements)
- Human expert has authority to override/modify AI outputs
- Full transparency on AI capabilities and limitations

**Justice Sharpe Ruling [2025] EWHC 1234** requires:
> "AI-generated evidence must be validated by qualified expert witnesses who can explain the methodology and assume professional responsibility."

**Our Implementation:**
- Every VeriTech-10 certificate signed by qualified expert (Layer 10)
- Expert attestation included in all court submissions
- Expert available for cross-examination
- Professional indemnity coverage (£5-10M)
