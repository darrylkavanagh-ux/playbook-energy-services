Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

What if you don't find any savings?

If we don't identify recoverable overcharges, you pay nothing. Our fee is only charged on actual savings recovered.

How long does an audit take?

Initial audits typically take 2-4 weeks depending on the complexity of your business and number of cost categories analyzed.

Do you handle the recovery process?

Yes, we manage all negotiations with suppliers and service providers to recover identified overcharges on your behalf.

What documentation do you need?

We typically need 24 months of bills, contracts, and service agreements for the categories being audited.

Can you audit just one cost category?

Absolutely. You can choose individual services like energy audits or opt for our comprehensive audit across all categories.

How do you ensure confidentiality?

All client information is protected under strict confidentiality agreements and our VeriTech 10 certification standards.
