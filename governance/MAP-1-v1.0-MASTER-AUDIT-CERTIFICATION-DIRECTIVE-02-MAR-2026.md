This directive establishes the permanent, non-negotiable framework for achieving and maintaining VeriTech Level 10 certification across every output, every engine, every repository, and every client engagement. Nothing leaves this platform uncertified. Nothing is relied upon without verification. Nothing is presented as fact without evidence.

## SECTION 2: VERITECH LEVEL 10 — THE 10-POINT PROTOCOL

### LEGAL FOUNDATION

**(a) EU AI Act — Regulation (EU) 2024/1689**
High-risk AI: conformity assessment, human oversight (Art 14), transparency (Art 50), risk management, documentation.
Penalties: €35 million or 7% global turnover.

**(b) Dame Victoria Sharp — [2025] EWHC 1383 (Admin)**
R (Ayinde) v London Borough of Haringey; Al-Haroun v Qatar National Bank. 6 June 2025. King's Bench Division.
- AI content MUST be disclosed to courts
- Human professional MUST verify all AI outputs
- Unverified AI content = contempt of court, perverting justice
- Criminal sanctions available for non-compliance

**(c) THE CONVERGENCE**
EU AI Act created the regulatory framework. Sharp created the professional obligation. Together: mandatory verification market. VeriTech Level 10 satisfies both simultaneously.

### THE 10 POINTS

**POINT 1 — SOURCE DATA INTEGRITY**
All source data authenticated. Chain of custody from intake to output. No data fabricated, estimated, or assumed. Every data point traceable to origin. SENTINEL assigns provenance grade. IRIS verifies document authenticity. Any data point without verifiable source: REJECTED.

**POINT 2 — AI ENGINE TRANSPARENCY**
Every engine used identified. Every engine's contribution documented. Engines NOT used stated with reason. Full Platform Resource Manifest produced.

**POINT 3 — CALCULATION VERIFICATION**
Every calculation independently verifiable. Formula shown. Inputs identified with source. No black-box arithmetic. Every intermediate step documented. SHERLOCK performs forensic arithmetic verification. VERITECH independently re-calculates.

**POINT 4 — LEGAL CITATION VERIFICATION (BLACKSTONE)**
Every legal citation verified against authoritative database. BAILII, Irish Legal, ECHR HUDOC, OSCOLA compliance. No hallucinated citations. Standard: Senior Partner at an internationally recognised law firm, or Senior/King's Counsel, would rely on it without reservation.

**POINT 5 — EVIDENCE GRADING (SENTINEL)**
Every factual claim graded:
- A1: Official verified record (court, regulatory, gazette)
- A2: Professional verified (audited accounts, certified survey)
- B1: Reliable public (Companies House, CRO, official statistics)
- B2: Credible secondary (quality journalism, academic)
- C: Single unconfirmed source requiring corroboration
- D: Unverified — NOT relied upon

**POINT 6 — CROSS-REFERENCE VERIFICATION**
All cross-references verified for consistency. Internal contradictions flagged. Any contradiction: HALTED — flagged for resolution.

**POINT 7 — AI CONTENT LABELLING (EU AI Act Article 50)**
ALL AI-assisted content clearly marked. Percentage of AI-generated vs human-authored stated. Disclosure statement on first page of every document.

**POINT 8 — HUMAN-IN-THE-LOOP PROFESSIONAL REVIEW**
Physical review by qualified professional. Sign-off is personal. Cannot be delegated to AI. Their review converts 98.5% AI verification to 100% GUARANTEED.

**POINT 9 — AUDIT TRAIL**
Full chain of custody. Every version tracked. Every edit logged. Every engine output preserved. Reproducible from raw inputs.

**POINT 10 — REGULATORY COMPLIANCE**
EU AI Act conformity assessment. Sharp ruling disclosure. GDPR compliance. Sector-specific: CRU (Ireland energy), Ofgem (UK energy), FCA (UK financial), CBI (Ireland financial), SRA/BSB (UK legal), Law Society of Ireland, ICO/DPC (data protection).
